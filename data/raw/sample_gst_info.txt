GST (Goods and Services Tax) Information for SMEs

What is GST?
GST is a comprehensive indirect tax on manufacture, sale and consumption of goods and services throughout India. It replaced multiple cascading taxes levied by the central and state governments.

GST Rates:
- 0% GST: Basic food items, books, newspapers
- 5% GST: Essential items like packaged food items, footwear below Rs 1000
- 12% GST: Computers, processed food, mobile phones below Rs 2000
- 18% GST: Most goods and services including restaurants with AC
- 28% GST: Luxury items like cars, tobacco, aerated drinks

GSTR-1 Filing:
GSTR-1 is monthly return showing outward supplies. Due date is 11th of next month.
Late fee: Rs 50 per day (maximum Rs 5000).

GSTR-3B Filing:
GSTR-3B is monthly summary return with tax payment. Due date is 20th of next month.
Late fee: Rs 50 per day for taxpayers with turnover above Rs 5 crore.
Late fee: Rs 20 per day for others.

Input Tax Credit:
Input tax credit can be claimed on goods and services used for business purposes.
Cannot claim ITC on motor cars, food and beverages, outdoor catering, beauty treatment.

Section 80C Deductions:
Maximum deduction allowed under Section 80C is Rs 1.5 lakh per financial year.
Eligible investments: PPF, ELSS, NSC, ULIP, Home loan principal, tuition fees.
